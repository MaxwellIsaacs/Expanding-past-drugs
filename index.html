Illegal Empires

A series of podcasts delving into the extensive influence of criminal networks on politics and industries like oil and agriculture, focusing on Brazil and Mexico. Listen as I explore their impact on economies and governance with experts in their field. Join me to learn more about these multi-billion dollar criminal networks

Gladys McCormick

Professor McCormick and I discuss how and why criminal operations have expanded into industries like oil and medicine, and the political implications of the Sinola and Jalisco New Generation organizations

Enrique Arias

Professor Arias and I discuss how DTOs have expanded into call centers, his trip to Brazil , and how the Brazillian police have been co-opted

Jessie Trudeau

Professor Trudeau and I discuss a few articles of hers about criminal governance in Brazil and gang-life looks in the favelas
