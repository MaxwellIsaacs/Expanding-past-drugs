Bio

Enrique Desmond Arias researches security and politics in Latin America and the Caribbean. He has written books like "Criminal Enterprises and Governance in Latin America and the Caribbean," "Violent Democracies in Latin America," and "Drugs and Democracy in Rio de Janeiro," focusing on crime, governance, and the impact of violence. His work shows how criminal groups influence politics and everyday life in cities like Rio de Janeiro, Medellin, and Kingston. Arias has received funding from several foundations and served as a consultant to international organizations, including the United Nations.
