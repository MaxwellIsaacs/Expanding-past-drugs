Illegal Empires

A podcast exploring the hidden reach of criminal networks into politics, oil, agriculture, and more, with an emphasis on Brazil and Mexico. We dissect their influence on economies and governance, offering straightforward insights into their complex impact. Join us for an unvarnished look at the unseen forces shaping global markets.

Gladys McCormick

Professor McCormick and I discuss how and why criminal operations have expanded into industries like oil and medicine, and the political implications of the Sinola and Hallisco New Generation organizations

Enrique Arias

Professor Arias and I discuss how DTOs have expanded into call centers, his trip to Brazil, and how the Brazillian police have been co-opted

Jessie Trudeau

Professor Trudeau and I discuss a few articles of hers about criminal governance in Brazil and gang-life looks in the favelas
